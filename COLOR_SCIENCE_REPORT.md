## 2. Optimization Strategy (Human Factors Engineering)

### Principles
1.  **Reduce Peak Luminance**: Move away from `#FFFFFF` (100% Lightness) to `#F9FAFB` or `#FDFDFE` to cut harsh blue/white emission.
2.  **Soften Black**: Move from `#000000` to `#111827` or `#1F1F1F`. Natural objects are rarely pure black; slightly lifted blacks feel more organic.
3.  **Optimal Contrast Zone**: Target **12:1 to 16:1** for long-form reading. 21:1 is too high (dazzling); 4.5:1 is the bare minimum (hard for some).
